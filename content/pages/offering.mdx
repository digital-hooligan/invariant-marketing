# Offering

## Positioning Statement

Invariant is an applied systems studio for structured execution.

## Who It’s For

- Founder-led teams
- Regulated or operationally complex organizations
- Teams shipping internal tools or applied AI

## What We Do

- Discovery and signal clarification
- Prototype and build increments
- Workflow automation
- Applied AI integrations (operator-led; no automation claims)

## What We Don’t Do

- Open-ended R&D
- Staff augmentation
- Marketing agency services
- Guaranteed outcomes

## Engagement Types

- Discovery
- Build sprint
- Incremental delivery
- Retainer

## Process Overview

- Scope is defined before work begins
- Milestones are tracked
- Change requests are logged
